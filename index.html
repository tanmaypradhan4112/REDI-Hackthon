About The Event

The draft consultation for "India Digital Ecosystem Architecture 2.0" was published in January 2022 by the Ministry of Electronics & IT (MeitY), Government of India. India's desire to converge digital technology as a single architecture was made clear by this action. Re-engineering the digital architecture to bring about the Digital Ecosystem is the necessary strategy at this point, as mass exclusion of individuals is occurring by default by design.

In this regard, Student Chapter of Swecha Telangana has planned to conduct RE-Engineering Digital India Hackathon, across the academic institutions in the state of Telangana.
